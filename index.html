About the Artist

Naz Basit

MBBS, RDMS Co President AAUW Mclean Chapter
"I found my love for art at a young age, initially starting with drawings and sketch-work. After a long hiatus to pursue my dream of becoming a physician, I have revatilized my passion and strive to own my craft. Khubsoorti is urdu for "beautiful", the language of Pakistan, my country of origin. You'll find the art of that region as the inspiration behind much of my artwork."
